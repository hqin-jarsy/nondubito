Essays in the Self-as-an-End Tradition B7 Han Qin (秦汉)

Philosophical essays that apply the Self-as-an-End framework to the lives and thought of philosophers across East and West — reading them not as historical curiosities, but as structural analyses of what it means to exist as an end in itself.

这些文章将 Self-as-an-End 框架应用于东西方哲学家的生命与思想—— 不是历史研究，而是结构性分析：在各自所处的本身而存在。
